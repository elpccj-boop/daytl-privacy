Daytl Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

Overview

Daytl is a puzzle game developed by Systecture. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

Daytl does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We do not require account creation or login.

Local Data

Your game progress, settings, and statistics are stored locally on your device only. This data is not transmitted to any server and is not accessible to us.

Advertising

Daytl displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain information for ad personalization, including:

  • Device identifiers
  • General location (country/region)
  • App usage data

You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking
  • Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads → Delete advertising ID

For more information about Google's data practices, visit: Google Privacy Policy

Children's Privacy

Daytl is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

Data Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of any data associated with your use of Daytl:

  • Local app data: Uninstall the app from your device to permanently remove all locally stored game progress, statistics, and preferences.
  • Advertising data: Data collected by Google AdMob can be managed through your Google account settings at Google Account Data & Privacy. You can also reset your advertising ID in your device settings.
  • Deletion requests: To request deletion of any data, email us at support@systecture.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at: support@systecture.com
